Faqay Foundation is a community-focused non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable individuals and communities.

Through education programs, humanitarian aid, healthcare initiatives, and sustainable development projects, the foundation works to create real opportunities and improve the wellbeing of communities.

About Us

Mission

To empower communities by providing access to education, humanitarian support, healthcare, and opportunities that improve social and economic wellbeing.

Vision

To build a society where every individual has equal opportunities to grow, learn, and contribute to community development.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Accountability
  • Community Empowerment
  • Transparency
  • Sustainability
  • Inclusiveness

Our Programs

Education Support

Providing school materials, mentorship, and learning opportunities for disadvantaged students.

Humanitarian Food Support

Providing food assistance and emergency relief support to vulnerable families.

Medical Support

Delivering healthcare assistance, medical checkups, and health awareness programs for vulnerable communities.

Clean Water Support

Helping communities access clean and safe drinking water through water supply initiatives.

Our Impact

  • Supporting education for disadvantaged students
  • Providing food support for vulnerable families
  • Delivering healthcare assistance
  • Improving access to clean water
  • Empowering communities through development programs
